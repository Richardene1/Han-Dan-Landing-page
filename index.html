Sustainability

What Sustainability Means to Us

At Han&Dan, our sustainability efforts are built on four key pillars that guide how we operate, grow, and deliver impact.

Pillar one ●

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to reducing the environmental footprint of oil and gas servicing through practical, measurable actions.

  • Eco-Friendly Procurement: We source equipment, tools, and materials from suppliers who follow environmentally responsible practices.
  • Emission Reduction: We prioritise technologies and service practices that minimise greenhouse gas emissions and energy waste.
  • Circularity in Operations: We advocate for reuse, recycling, and responsible waste disposal within circular economy principles.
  • Compliance with Global Standards: Our operations align with ISO 14001 and ISO 50001.
Pillar two ●

Social Responsibility

Han&Dan's operations are anchored in people and communities.

  • Local Content Development: We invest in Nigerian and African talent by prioritising indigenous suppliers and subcontractors.
  • Capacity Building: We train and mentor young engineers and technicians.
  • Health & Safety: We uphold the highest HSE standards to protect employees, contractors and host communities.
  • Community Impact: We support educational programs, healthcare access, and sustainable local businesses.
Pillar three ●

Governance & Integrity

Sustainability also means strong governance structures that build trust.

  • Transparency: Honest communication with stakeholders and regulators.
  • Ethical Procurement: Monitoring supply chains for anti-corruption and labour standards.
  • Accountability: Reporting aligned with GRI and SDG frameworks.
  • Partnerships: Working only with organisations that share sustainability values.
Pillar four ●

Innovation & Future Outlook

Han&Dan is not only a supplier but also a driver of innovation in sustainable energy servicing.

  • Energy Transition Support: Expanding services into renewable energy integration.
  • Digitalization for Sustainability: Using AI and data-driven platforms for efficiency and compliance.
  • Regional Expansion: Scaling across Africa with sustainability at the core of growth.
